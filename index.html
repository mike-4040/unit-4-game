Cristal Collector

You will be given the Goal - a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to the Score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching the Score to the Goal. You lost the game if the Score goes above the Goal.
